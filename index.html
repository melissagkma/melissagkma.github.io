3D Simulator Room

In this room you and a few friends can experience what it's like front and center on stage during a

concert! There is a hug set up of a DJ turn table in which you can demonstrate your skills to your friends.

The room is equiped with 4 floor to ceiling monitors that stimulates a real life crowd of screaming people!

Build It Room

Enter the Build It Room to learn how to make your own instruments with materials found around the house!

Build your own mini guitar made from a tissue box and some rubber bands. Everything you make in this room

you can take home and show your friends and family!

Fashion

This room is filled with mannequins wearing the most bizarre outfits! Take a look at the fasion choices from

the 50s, 60s, 70s, 80s, 90s, and today! You will see the differences in the fashion choices up to now!
