Welcome to MusicWorld

MusicWorld has activities from learning the history to building your own instrument!

Click to find a sneak preview of each room!

3D Simulator Room

In this room you and a few friends can experience what it's like

front and center on stage during a and center on stage during a

concert! There is a huge set up of a DJ turn table in which you

can demonstrate your skills to your friends.The room is equipped

with 4 floor to ceiling monitors that stimulates a real life crowd!

Jam Room

You and your friends can enter this room, choose an instrument

each and jam to the notes on the screen! The computer will

process your scores and show on the screen!

Build It Room

Enter the Build It Room to learn how to make your own instruments

with materials found around the house! Build your own mini guitar

made from a tissue box and some rubber bands. Everything you make

in this room you can take home and show your friends and family!

Fashion

This room is filled with mannequins wearing the most bizarre outfits!

outfits! Take a look at the fasion choices from the 70s, 80s, 90s,

and today! You will see the differences in the fashion choices up to now!
